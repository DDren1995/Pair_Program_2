Green River Community College's main campus was founded in Auburn, Washington in 1965. Green River College is a public college where students from many different countries come together to learn and pursue interests. Green River's main campus occupies 180 acres of beautifully forested land. There are additional branch campuses located in downtown Auburn, Kent and Enumclaw. The combination of strong academics and a lively campus allow Green River to provide students a competent education with lessons that will stay with them throughout their careers.
